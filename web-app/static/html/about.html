About Us

DestinyHub is a news, community, and portal fansite dedicated to Bungie's upcoming game franchise, Destiny. We aim to keep you up to date with everything Destiny by promoting the best content from around the community. We also host a forum where you can discuss game news, share your artwork, find allies for your adventures, or just chat about whatever.
